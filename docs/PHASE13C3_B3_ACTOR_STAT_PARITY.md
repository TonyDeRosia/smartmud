## Gameplay consumer matrix

Active secondary stats have canonical consumers: accuracy/hit_bonus/evasion feed hit resolution; attack_power/damage_bonus feed physical damage; spell_power feeds spell damage; healing_power feeds healing; armor feeds mitigation; critical ratings/avoidance/damage feed critical resolution; saves feed save resolution; resistances feed typed mitigation; resource maxima feed explicit maxima refresh/clamping; carrying values feed encumbrance.

## Speed and presence decisions

Initiative, attack_speed, casting_speed, recovery_speed, and movement_speed are currently calculated for detailed diagnostics but marked inactive with the reason: `Combat rounds do not currently use initiative ordering or speed-based timing.` Presence/charisma remains an active primary attribute; current combat consumers are empty and the snapshot records `combat_consumed=false`.

Not performed in this Linux container.
