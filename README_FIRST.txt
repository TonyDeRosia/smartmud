Adventurer's Guild AI - Quick Start

1. Double-click AdventurerGuildAI.exe to play.
2. Click New Campaign to begin, or Campaigns to continue an existing campaign.
3. Type what your character does and press Send.
4. Campaigns autosave automatically after turns.
5. AI and image settings are optional and can be changed later in Settings.

You do not need to set up local AI tools or image generation to reach the play screen.
